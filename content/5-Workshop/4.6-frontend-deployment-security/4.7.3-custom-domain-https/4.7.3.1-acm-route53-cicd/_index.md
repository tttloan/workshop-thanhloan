#### Content

1. Create a Hosted Zone in Route 53 for the domain.
2. Retrieve the list of Nameservers (NS) from the Hosted Zone.
3. Go to Name.com and update the Nameservers to point to Route 53.
4. Confirm that DNS propagation is complete.
